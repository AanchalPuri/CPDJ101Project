What is a DeepFake?

DeepFakes are essentially AI generated images made to look like real people. This can be used to impersonate someone or depict them doing things theey wouldn't normally do. DeepFakes also aren't limited to just images. You can make videos and voice deepfakes as well which can allow you to for example, make the president run away from the police, or put someone in any clothing of your choice. Unfortunately that second example is more real than you would think.

In the News

Non-consesual Images

One of the examples how DeepFakes harm people is through creating images of people doing things they would never actually do. This even extends to sexual content of women and even young girls.

The Scam Revolution

Because of how accurate and realistic it can be, deepfakes have also found a home with scammers who use the technology to take money or information from others. These scams can take many forms. Scamers can use the faces of celebrities by making it look like they endorsed a product they never acutally endorsed. The victims end up buying a fake product and get their finacial inforamtion stolen. Scamers can also deepfakes in romance scams where they use dating sites along with fake photos to get information and money from people by pretending to be someone else. They can even use deepfakes to create false trageties and elict money through donations.
